## **宋子帆**
<hr />
<p style="margin-bottom:0.8cm; margin-left: 0.5cm"> </p>
<p class="bold-text">
I am a Ph.D candidate in Computer Science at Tongji University, luckily advised by Prof. <a href="https://vill-lab.github.io/" target="_blank" class="hl-text">Cairong Zhao</a> and <a href="https://huguosheng.github.io/" target="_blank" class="hl-text">Guosheng Hu</a>. Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor degree in Computer Science from Tongji University. I had a remarkable journey at Microsoft Research Asia (mentored by <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=LLk9dR8AAAAJ" target="_blank" class="hl-text">Kaitao Song</a> and <a href="http://recmind.cn/" target="_blank" class="hl-text">Dongsheng Li</a>) and OpenMMLab of Shanghai AI Laboratory (mentored by <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=QDXADSEAAAAJ" target="_blank" class="hl-text">Wenwei Zhang</a> and <a href="https://chenkai.site/" target="_blank" class="hl-text">Kai Chen</a>). My research interests lie in <span class="hl-title">Multimodal Learning</span>, <span class="hl-title">Data-centric AI</span>, and <span class="hl-title">LLM Agent</span>.
</p>
